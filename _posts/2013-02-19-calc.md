		<h2>calc</h2>
	</header>
	<p class="feature__description">
		Method of allowing calculated values for length units, i.e. width: calc(100% - 3em)
	</p>
<pre class="feature__code"><code>
.example {
  width: 500px; /* Fallback! **/
  width: -webkit-calc(50% - 20px);
  width:    -moz-calc(50% - 20px);
  width:         calc(50% - 20px);
}
</code></pre>
